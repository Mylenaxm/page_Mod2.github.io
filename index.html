Christmas Traditions

Watch a Christmas Movie

Going out (or staying in) to watch movies together at Christmas is one of the most popular Christmas traditions for many families.

Bake Christmas Cookies

Making cookies, baking pies, or building gingerbread houses falls in line with the holiday spirit that it is better to give than receive.

Hide An Elf on The Shelf

The tradition has become extremely popular with adults and children alike, as the Elf hides in a new place around the house every morning and the children try to find him.
